# Minecraft Crafting Recipes

## Essential Tools

### Wooden Tools
- **Wooden Pickaxe**: 3 planks (top row) + 2 sticks (center column)
- **Wooden Axe**: 2 planks (top-left + mid-left) + 1 plank (top-center) + 2 sticks (center column)
- **Wooden Sword**: 2 planks (top two center) + 1 stick (bottom center)
- **Wooden Shovel**: 1 plank (top center) + 2 sticks (center column)
- **Wooden Hoe**: 2 planks (top row, 2 slots) + 2 sticks (center column)

### Stone Tools
Same patterns as wooden but replace planks with cobblestone.

### Iron Tools
Same patterns but use iron ingots instead of planks.

### Diamond Tools
Same patterns but use diamonds instead of planks.

### Netherite Tools
Upgrade any diamond tool at a Smithing Table with a Netherite Upgrade template + netherite ingot.

## Tool Tiers (Durability / Speed)
1. **Wood**: 59 durability, slowest
2. **Stone**: 131 durability
3. **Iron**: 250 durability
4. **Diamond**: 1561 durability
5. **Netherite**: 2031 durability, fastest, fire resistant

## Important Crafting Recipes

### Furnace
8 cobblestone in a ring (center empty)

### Crafting Table
4 planks in 2x2 grid

### Chest
8 planks in a ring

### Bed
3 wool (top row) + 3 planks (bottom row)

### Torch
1 coal/charcoal (top) + 1 stick (bottom)

### Enchanting Table
1 book (top center) + 2 diamonds (left and right middle) + 4 obsidian (bottom row + center)

### Anvil
3 iron blocks (top row) + 1 iron ingot (center) + 3 iron ingots (bottom row)

### Brewing Stand
1 blaze rod (top center) + 3 cobblestone (bottom row)

### Shield
1 iron ingot (top center) + 6 planks (surrounding)

### Compass
4 iron ingots (cardinal directions) + 1 redstone (center)

### Clock
4 gold ingots (cardinal directions) + 1 redstone (center)

### Map
8 paper (ring) + 1 compass (center)

### Book
3 paper + 1 leather

### Bookshelf
6 planks (top and bottom rows) + 3 books (middle row)

## Enchantments

### Weapon Enchantments
- **Sharpness (I-V)**: +1.25 damage per level
- **Smite (V)**: Extra damage to undead
- **Bane of Arthropods (V)**: Extra damage to spiders
- **Fire Aspect (II)**: Sets target on fire
- **Looting (III)**: More mob drops
- **Knockback (II)**: Increased knockback

### Tool Enchantments
- **Efficiency (I-V)**: Faster mining
- **Fortune (III)**: More drops from ores
- **Silk Touch**: Mine blocks in original form
- **Unbreaking (III)**: Increased durability
- **Mending**: Repair with XP orbs

### Armor Enchantments
- **Protection (IV)**: General damage reduction
- **Fire Protection (IV)**: Fire damage reduction
- **Blast Protection (IV)**: Explosion damage reduction
- **Projectile Protection (IV)**: Ranged damage reduction
- **Thorns (III)**: Reflects damage to attackers
- **Feather Falling (IV)**: Fall damage reduction (boots only)

### Bow Enchantments
- **Power (V)**: Increased arrow damage
- **Punch (II)**: Increased knockback
- **Flame**: Fire arrows
- **Infinity**: Infinite arrows (need 1 arrow)

## Smelting

### Ores
- Iron Ore → Iron Ingot (furnace)
- Gold Ore → Gold Ingot (furnace)
- Copper Ore → Copper Ingot (furnace)
- Ancient Debris → Netherite Scrap (blast furnace recommended)
- 4 Netherite Scraps + 4 Gold Ingots → 1 Netherite Ingot

### Food
- Raw Beef → Steak
- Raw Chicken → Cooked Chicken
- Raw Porkchop → Cooked Porkchop
- Raw Cod → Cooked Cod
- Raw Salmon → Cooked Salmon
