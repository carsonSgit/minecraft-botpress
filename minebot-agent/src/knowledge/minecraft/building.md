# Minecraft Building Tips

## Block Palettes by Style

### Medieval
- **Walls**: Cobblestone, Stone Bricks, Mossy Stone Bricks, Deepslate Bricks
- **Roof**: Dark Oak Planks, Spruce Planks, Dark Oak Stairs/Slabs
- **Accents**: Oak Logs (pillars), Stripped Oak Logs, Iron Bars (windows)
- **Floor**: Stone Bricks, Polished Andesite, Oak Planks

### Modern
- **Walls**: White Concrete, Light Gray Concrete, Quartz Block, Smooth Quartz
- **Accents**: Black Concrete, Gray Concrete, Glass Panes
- **Floor**: Polished Blackstone, Dark Oak Planks, Smooth Stone
- **Details**: Sea Lanterns, End Rods, Item Frames

### Japanese
- **Walls**: White Concrete, Birch Planks, Spruce Planks
- **Roof**: Deepslate Tiles, Blackstone, Nether Brick Stairs
- **Structure**: Dark Oak Logs (pillars), Spruce Fences
- **Garden**: Moss Block, Azalea, Bamboo, Water features

### Rustic/Cottage
- **Walls**: Stripped Birch Logs, White Terracotta, Cobblestone
- **Roof**: Spruce Stairs/Slabs
- **Accents**: Flower Pots, Lanterns, Trapdoors (shutters)
- **Garden**: Composters, Hay Bales, Barrels

### Fantasy/Magical
- **Walls**: Purpur Block, End Stone Bricks, Prismarine
- **Accents**: Amethyst, Crying Obsidian, Soul Lanterns
- **Lighting**: End Rods, Soul Torches, Shroomlight
- **Details**: Enchanting Tables, Brewing Stands, Bookshelves

## Decorative Blocks

### Lighting
- **Torch**: Light level 14
- **Lantern**: Light level 15 (brightest placeable)
- **Sea Lantern**: Light level 15, underwater-friendly
- **Glowstone**: Light level 15
- **Shroomlight**: Light level 15, organic look
- **End Rod**: Light level 14, slim decorative pillar
- **Candles (1-4)**: Light level 3-12
- **Froglight**: Light level 15 (Pearlescent, Verdant, Ochre variants)

### Storage & Functional
- **Chest**: 27 slots, can double up
- **Barrel**: 27 slots, directional, won't block when covered
- **Shulker Box**: Portable chest, keeps items when broken
- **Ender Chest**: Shared inventory across all ender chests per player

### Vegetation
- **Flowers**: Dandelion, Poppy, Cornflower, Lily of Valley, Azure Bluet, etc.
- **Tall Plants**: Sunflower, Rose Bush, Peony, Lilac
- **Trees**: Oak, Spruce, Birch, Jungle, Acacia, Dark Oak, Mangrove, Cherry
- **Special**: Azalea (flowering), Dripleaf, Moss, Glow Lichen

## Building Techniques

### Depth and Detail
- Use stairs and slabs to add depth to walls
- Mix similar blocks (stone bricks + cracked stone bricks + mossy stone bricks)
- Add trapdoors as window shutters
- Use walls and fences for railings
- Item frames and armor stands for decoration

### Roofing
- Use stairs for main slope
- Add slabs at roof ridge
- Overhang the roof 1 block past walls
- Mix in upside-down stairs for eave detail

### Landscaping
- Vary terrain height (don't build on flat ground)
- Use coarse dirt and path blocks around buildings
- Add custom trees (bigger than default)
- Water features with half-slabs for shallow water

### Color Palettes (Concrete)
All 16 concrete colors available:
- **Warm**: Red, Orange, Yellow, Brown
- **Cool**: Blue, Light Blue, Cyan, Purple
- **Neutral**: White, Light Gray, Gray, Black
- **Nature**: Green, Lime, Magenta, Pink

### Interior Design
- Use carpet on floors for color
- Banners as wall decorations
- Flower pots on tables (fence + pressure plate)
- Paintings for wall art
- Maps in item frames for custom art
