# Minecraft Mobs

## Hostile Mobs (Overworld)

### Zombie
- **Spawn**: Dark areas (light level 0), nighttime
- **Drops**: Rotten Flesh, Iron Ingot (rare), Carrot (rare), Potato (rare)
- **Behavior**: Attacks players and villagers, burns in sunlight
- **Variants**: Baby Zombie (faster), Husk (desert, no sunlight burn), Drowned (underwater)

### Skeleton
- **Spawn**: Dark areas (light level 0), nighttime
- **Drops**: Bones, Arrows, Bow (rare)
- **Behavior**: Shoots arrows, burns in sunlight
- **Variant**: Stray (snowy biomes, shoots slowness arrows)

### Creeper
- **Spawn**: Dark areas (light level 0), nighttime
- **Drops**: Gunpowder, Music Disc (when killed by skeleton)
- **Behavior**: Silently approaches and explodes. Does NOT burn in sunlight.

### Spider
- **Spawn**: Dark areas (light level 0), nighttime
- **Drops**: String, Spider Eye
- **Behavior**: Hostile at night, neutral during day. Can climb walls.
- **Variant**: Cave Spider (smaller, poisonous, found in mineshafts)

### Enderman
- **Spawn**: Dark areas, all dimensions
- **Drops**: Ender Pearl
- **Behavior**: Neutral until looked at. Teleports. Damaged by water.

### Witch
- **Spawn**: Dark areas, swamp huts
- **Drops**: Various potions, Glowstone Dust, Redstone, Sugar, Spider Eye
- **Behavior**: Throws harmful potions, drinks defensive potions

### Phantom
- **Spawn**: Above players who haven't slept for 3+ in-game days
- **Drops**: Phantom Membrane (used for Slow Falling potions and elytra repair)

### Slime
- **Spawn**: Swamp biomes at night, specific underground chunks
- **Drops**: Slimeball
- **Behavior**: Splits into smaller slimes when killed

## Hostile Mobs (Nether)

### Ghast
- **Spawn**: Nether Wastes, Soul Sand Valley, Basalt Deltas
- **Drops**: Ghast Tear, Gunpowder
- **Behavior**: Flies, shoots fireballs (can be deflected)

### Blaze
- **Spawn**: Nether Fortresses
- **Drops**: Blaze Rod (essential for brewing and End portal)
- **Behavior**: Flies, shoots fire charges in volleys

### Wither Skeleton
- **Spawn**: Nether Fortresses
- **Drops**: Coal, Bones, Wither Skeleton Skull (rare, needed for Wither boss)
- **Behavior**: Inflicts Wither effect on hit

### Magma Cube
- **Spawn**: Nether Wastes, Basalt Deltas
- **Drops**: Magma Cream
- **Behavior**: Like slimes but fire-resistant, splits on death

### Piglin Brute
- **Spawn**: Bastion Remnants
- **Drops**: Golden Axe (rare)
- **Behavior**: Always hostile, cannot be distracted with gold

### Hoglin
- **Spawn**: Crimson Forest
- **Drops**: Raw Porkchop, Leather
- **Behavior**: Hostile, afraid of warped fungus and nether portals

## Hostile Mobs (End)

### Ender Dragon
- **Spawn**: The End (boss)
- **Drops**: Massive XP, Dragon Egg (first kill)
- **Defeat**: Destroy End Crystals on obsidian towers, then attack dragon

### Shulker
- **Spawn**: End Cities
- **Drops**: Shulker Shell (used to craft Shulker Boxes)
- **Behavior**: Shoots homing projectiles that cause Levitation

## Passive Mobs

### Farm Animals
- **Cow**: Drops Raw Beef, Leather. Milk with bucket.
- **Pig**: Drops Raw Porkchop. Rideable with saddle + carrot on stick.
- **Chicken**: Drops Raw Chicken, Feathers. Lays eggs.
- **Sheep**: Drops Wool (shear for more). Dye-able in 16 colors.
- **Horse**: Tameable, rideable with saddle. Various speeds and jump heights.

### Villagers
- **Trades**: Each profession has unique trades
- **Professions**: Farmer, Librarian, Armorer, Weaponsmith, Toolsmith, Cleric, Butcher, Leatherworker, Fletcher, Cartographer, Shepherd, Mason, Fisherman, Nitwit (no trades)
- **Breeding**: Need beds and food (bread, carrots, potatoes, beetroots)
- **Iron Golem**: Spawns naturally to protect villages (3+ villagers, 3+ beds)

### Useful Passive Mobs
- **Wolf**: Tamed with bones. Attacks your targets.
- **Cat**: Tamed with raw fish. Scares creepers and phantoms.
- **Bee**: Pollinates crops (faster growth). Produces honey.
- **Axolotl**: Aquatic ally, attacks hostile water mobs.
